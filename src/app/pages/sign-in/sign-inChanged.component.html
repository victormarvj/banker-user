Online Safety Tips

At WovenTallowHold, your online security is our top priority. To ensure you have a safe and secure experience, please keep the following tips in mind whenever accessing your account or performing online transactions.

Keep your login credentials private: Your Account Access ID and Password should never be shared with anyone. Always use strong, unique passwords.

Be aware of your surroundings: Make sure no one is watching when entering your login details, especially in public places.

Protect your PIN: Never write it down or disclose it to anyone under any circumstances.

Access your account safely: Always log in via our official website only: https://dashboard.woventallowhold.com

Always log out properly: Use the ‘Log Out’ button after every session. Avoid accessing your account on shared or public computers. If you do, clear your browser’s cache and history after logging out.
